Team's History

The team's last year project was to use the CanSat satellite as a Wi-Fi server and take several measurements, such as Ultraviolet Light,altitude and radioactivity. The measurements were then uploaded to our old website.Our mission this year is to make two CanSat satellites, by inserting a smaller one in our main CanSat, both taking several measurements. The results will then be compared and displayed on our website through the use of diagrams.
